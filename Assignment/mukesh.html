Mukesh Ambani Profile

Mukesh Ambani

Mukesh Dhirubhai Ambani is an Indian billionaire businessman. He is currently the chairman and managing director of Reliance Industries, India's most valuable company by market value. With an estimated net worth of $87.2 billion as of May 2023, he is the richest person in Asia and 13th richest in the world..
